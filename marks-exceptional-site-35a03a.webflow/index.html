Next-Gen Capabilities at Your Fingertips

RemoteHQ is a platform created by a passionate team based in New Zealand, dedicated to transforming forestry monitoring with smart, practical technology. Born from years of hands-on experience and a commitment to simplifying forestry compliance, we build tools that empower you to protect the environment while working smarter and faster.

Standardised Block Details

View forestry operations live, with a consistent and standardised approach across regions.

Wov inspection timeline

Monitor and support forestry blocks through the critical ‘Window of Vulnerability’ phase with clear, scheduled inspections.

Compliance Reporting

Generate comprehensive PDF reports aligned with NES-CF notifications and consent conditions.

Slash identification

Upload drone and panoramic imagery to identify slash and evaluate the risk of it entering waterways.

de-risk blocks

Reduce risk with nationally consistent yet regionally specific checklists of Areas of Good Practice (AOGPs) and Areas of Concern (AOCs).

staff training library

Onboard new team members using clear training guidelines and hundreds of practical examples of common remedial tasks.

lidar assessments

Leverage mobile phone LiDAR technology to accurately assess stream crossings.
