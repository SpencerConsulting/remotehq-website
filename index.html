RemoteHQ - Environmental Performance

RemoteHQ provides Regional Councils, the forestry industry, and the public with better insights into forestry activity in their regions.

Our platform simplifies complex monitoring processes, offering clear, actionable feedback on areas of concern and highlighting areas of good practice. It also identifies current and potential land movement within recently harvested blocks, helping stakeholders make informed decisions.

RemoteHQ delivers insights across multiple scales, enabling users to understand the potential impacts of forest harvests, both immediate and long-term.

As we face increasingly intense storm events and growing risks during the post-harvest 'window of vulnerability,' comprehensive oversight of forestry blocks has never been more critical. RemoteHQ empowers users with the tools needed to navigate these challenges and ensure the stability and sustainability of forestry operations.
